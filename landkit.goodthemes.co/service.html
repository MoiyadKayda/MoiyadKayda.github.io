Taxation Services

  • Direct Tax compliance services; i.e. Consulting, Representation in scrutiny and appeal cases, Tax Deduction at Source (TDS) and e-filing TDS returns, Wealth Tax, International Taxation (w.r.t. withholding taxes on cross-border transactions) etc.
  • Providing Indirect Tax consultation and compliance services; i.e. providing consultancy on critical issues under indirect taxes, preparing and filing periodic returns, representation before the authorities, assistance in refund and rebate claims and partial and/or complete outsourcing of direct and indirect tax compliances.

  • Fixed asset verification and certification for multinational companies.
